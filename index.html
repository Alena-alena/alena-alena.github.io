Frequently Asked Questions

Why would I be interested in selling my points?

Selling your points can deliver more value for you. Banks offer half a penny for each hard-earned point you have. By selling your points, you can receive cash within just a few hours. Our goal is to bridge the gap between people individuals and businesses with excess points earned for business travel, first-class travel, and card membership. By brokering your points safely and securely, we can afford better rates in the reward points industry.
How do I know the process is safe?

We use technologically innovative solutions to protect your information. We work carefully and diligently to make sure your private information is kept confidential. These efforts earned us a #1 spot in customer satisfaction, and we have more repeat customers than any mileage broker in the industry.
This is my first time selling points. How does it work?

It's simple. Fill out the form, get an instant quote, accept the offer, and get paid!
How will I get paid?

We use PayPal for all points payments. If you use Amex, Chase, or Citi, you'll be paid via eCheck.
How long does it take to use my points?

Amex and Chase points get used in a few days.
Is this legal?

Yes. There is no law restricting the sale of your points, though airlines would prefer you used the points with them instead of a broker.
Why should I sell my Amex points?

American Express offers a .6% value to redeem your points for statement credits. You can sell them for a higher value than Amex offers.
Do you use my American Express Rewards points right away?

Amex points are generally used within a week.
What is an eCheck?

An eCheck is a check sent to your email. You print it out at home or work. From there you can follow your bank's instructions for a deposit, either via mobile device or at a branch.
